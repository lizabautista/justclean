Wash, Dry & Fold JustClean Laundry Shop is a service you can trust.

JUSTCLEAN laundry shop will be offering washing, drying and folding of clothes, pickup and delivery of clothes wherein they can pay through COD (cash on delivery), and online booking of schedule.

There will be membership form for regular customers which will they will have a discount to our services and regular pickups and deliveries. Coin operated machines are also available at the place and there will be a coffee station available at the store and a table for laptops and it has free WiFi.
